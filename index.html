What is gatekeep for?

Gatekeep is a Discord server with an ever-growing list of homestays. You can find a homestay you want to stay at, contact them, and book directly. Don't worry! We only add a homestay after thoroughly checking their documents. Plus, most of our homestays are also listed on other major websites. All of your other FAQs are answered on our Discord server.

How to join Gatekeep community?

Right now, we're expanding in two countries: India and Canada. It's free to join our Discord-IN and Discord-CA servers. However, once we reach critical mass, new users will need to subscribe to our Patreon for a very small fee to receive a link to our Discord server with homestay listings.
